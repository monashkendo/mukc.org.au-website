Regular Training#

You can find us training in Monash Sport, 42 Scenic Boulevard, Monash University Clayton Campus at the following times:

  • Monday 6:00 pm - 8:00 pm (Games Hall)
  • Wednesday 6:00 pm - 8:00 pm (Games Hall)
  • Saturday 9:00 am - 10:30 am (Stadium) - senior members (training in full armour) only
 

Competition season extra training#

During competition season, we have extra trainings. Please check the homepage and Facebook group for the latest information regarding extra trainings.

Closed dates#

During the year, the club closes for holidays, usually during Christmas break and some University holidays. Please check the homepage and Facebook group for the latest information regarding closed dates.
