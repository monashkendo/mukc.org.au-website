Regular Training#

Tip

Please double check our Facebook group for any last-minute changes to the below training times. This is especially the case if the date is near

  • Conflicting VKR and AKR Kendo events, e.g. yearly Nittaidai visit
  • Public holidays
  • University holidays
  • Public emergencies, e.g. COVID lockdowns

You can find us training in Monash Sport, 42 Scenic Boulevard, Monash University Clayton Campus at the following times:

  • Monday 6:00 pm - 8:00 pm (Games Hall)
  • Wednesday 6:00 pm - 8:00 pm (Games Hall)
  • Saturday 9:00 am - 10:30 am (Stadium) - senior members (training in full armour) only
 



How to get there#

The Monash Univeristy website has more information about Clayton Campus, and how to get there by public transport and inter-campus bus service.


Closed dates#

During the year, the club closes for holidays, usually during Christmas break and some University holidays. Please check the Facebook group for the latest information regarding closed dates.
