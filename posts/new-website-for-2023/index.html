New website for 2023

2023-01-12

New year, new website!

This site will now be more of a landing page for people searching for Monash Uni Kendo Club, and so will not be updated as regularly as the old site (that used to be a blog). Instead, please go to our Facebook page for the latest news. General information, and mostly-unchanging information about the club, such as training times will remain here. Major club news will still be posted here, such as - new website! :) Any comments, please send to website-admin@monashunikendoclub.org. See you at training!
