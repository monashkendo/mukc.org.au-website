Beginner?

Tip

More information about the 2023 beginner’s course will be available here soon. Please come back later!

Are you a complete beginner with no Kendo experience? Great! We hold a 2 week beginner’s course every year, commencing first of Semester 1 - usually around February or March.

No experience necessary! All you need is comfortable workout clothes! Beginners of any age are welcome! For juniors (under 16) we will need your parent/guardian to attend registration.

Not so beginner
If you have Kendo experience, and wish to join our club, please come to training and speak to one of the members. They will get you in touch with a committee member.
